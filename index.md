# 🌿 WisFlow  
### Wisdom that flows with you

---

## ✨ What is WisFlow?

WisFlow is your personal space for **spiritual wisdom, reflection, and calm clarity**.

Discover meaningful insights from the scriptures you connect with, understand their deeper context, and reflect in your own language — at your own pace.

No noise. No overwhelm. Just thoughtful moments that help you pause and grow.

---

## 🧘‍♂️ Why WisFlow?

In a busy world, wisdom often feels distant.

WisFlow brings it closer — in a way that is:
- Simple  
- Personal  
- Calm  
- Reflective  

It’s not about reading more.  
It’s about **understanding deeply**.

---

Select a scripture that resonates with you:

- Bhagavad Gita
